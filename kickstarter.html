Our Story

Personal safety devices fail for one reason: they can be taken away.

Your phone? Gone. Your purse? Gone. That panic button on your keychain? They took your keys.

We asked a simple question: Where won't anyone look?

ASSured Safety is the first personal safety device designed to stay with you when everything else is taken. Covert GPS tracking and silent panic alerts, hidden in the one place no attacker will check.

This isn't a joke product. This is real technology solving a real problem. The playful branding makes it approachable, but the engineering is deadly serious.

Rest ASSured. Help is on the way.

Questions?

How does the GPS tracking work?
The device contains a low-power GPS/cellular chip that reports location to our secure servers. The companion app shows real-time location and allows trusted contacts to track you. Battery lasts 5-7 days on a single charge.
Is it actually comfortable?
Extremely. The silicone models use medical-grade platinum-cured silicone (the same material used in medical implants). The tapered design and flared base make it comfortable for extended wear. Multiple sizes available.
How do I trigger a panic alert?
Three ways: (1) Squeeze pattern on the device itself, (2) Emergency button in the app, (3) Voice command through paired earbuds. Your emergency contacts receive your GPS location instantly.
What about the Plug N Puck system?
Pucks are decorative magnetic attachments that snap onto the base of any device. They're also sold separately at mainstream retailers (no age restriction for pucks alone) as a viral entry point. When someone sees a friend's cool puck, they ask where it came from.
When will devices ship?
Estimated delivery is Q3 2026. Early bird backers ship first. We'll provide regular manufacturing updates throughout the campaign and production process.
